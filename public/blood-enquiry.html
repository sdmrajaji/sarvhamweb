Guidelines for Blood Seekers

  • Accurate Information: Double-check the contact details of the requester and make sure phone lines are active.
  • Emergency Target: State the exact hospital name and specific ward number for rapid local coordination.
  • No Charges: Sarvham Foundation connects voluntary donors entirely free of cost. Never pay anyone for blood donation.
  • Immediate Support: For ultra-critical requirements needing immediate action under 2 hours, please call our emergency support directly instead of only submitting forms.
